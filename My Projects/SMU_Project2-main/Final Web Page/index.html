Restaurant Inspection Scores

This project pulls data from the Dallas Open Data that has over 50,000 health inspections and scores in the Dallas area. The data dates back to October of 2016. With this data we are communicating the Name of the Establishment, Physical Location, the Overall Inspections Scores, and Point deduction of individual violations.
